## 🕹️ How to Play (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Select Difficulty
- Easy: Random, slow, good for learning
- Medium: Balanced, strategic
- Hard: Fast, challenging

### Step 2: Build Your Deck
- Choose exactly 8 cards from 16 available
- Mix cards of different costs (2-7 elixir)
- Aim for 3.5-4.0 average elixir cost

### Step 3: Battle
- Play cards by clicking them (need enough elixir)
- Troops move automatically to attack
- Destroy 2 Princess Towers to unlock King Tower
- Destroy King Tower to win
- Or have more tower HP when 3 minutes expires

### Step 4: Results
- See if you won, lost, or tied
- Click "Play Again" for next match

**Total match time: 3 minutes**

## ✨ Key Features

### Real-Time Combat
- Units move and attack automatically
- Towers shoot at incoming enemies
- Spells cast instantly with AOE effects
- Multiple units can target same enemy

### Strategic Elixir System
- Start with 10 elixir
- Regenerates 1 per second
- 2 per second in last minute (double elixir)
- Each card costs 2-7 elixir

### AI at 3 Difficulty Levels
- **Easy:** Plays randomly, slow (5-8s between plays)
- **Medium:** Strategic, moderate (3-5s between plays)
- **Hard:** Advanced tactics, fast (2-3s between plays)

### Tower Defense Mechanics
- 3 towers per side (2 princess + 1 king)
- Destroy both princess towers = king tower vulnerable
- King tower HP: 3500
- Princess tower HP: 1800 each
