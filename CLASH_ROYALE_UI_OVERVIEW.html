🎯 Core Components

📊 TopHUD

Enemy/Player tower display with 3 towers each (King + 2 Princesses), HP bars, and large gold timer with pulsing animation.

⚡ BottomHUD

Elixir bar with 10-segment grid, smooth regeneration animation, color-based phase indicator (Green → Yellow → Red).

🎴 CardHand

4-card interactive hand with drag & drop, rarity badges, hover effects, and disabled states for insufficient elixir.

🎮 Arena

Central gameplay area (600×800px) with river divider, units, HP bars, damage popups, and drop zone validation.

🏆 MatchResult

Victory/Defeat screen with animated entrance, tower comparison stats, and "Play Again" / "Menu" buttons.

🎯 GameStates

Complete match flow: Difficulty Selection → Deck Builder → Battle → Result Screen.
