# 📺 Video Script: 4-Card Hand + Deck Cycling System (2–3 min)

## Scene 1: Intro (0:00–0:10)
**Visual:** Title screen, Clash Royale logo

**Voiceover:**
"Clash Royale just got smarter. We're introducing the 4-card hand system with full deck cycling. Here's what's changed."

**On-Screen:** Text: "4-Card Hand • Strategic Cycling • Live Balance Feedback"

---

## Scene 2: The Problem with All-8 Cards (0:10–0:25)
**Visual:** Old system showing all 8 cards

**Voiceover:**
"Previously, your entire 8-card deck was visible. You could play any card anytime. That's a lot of information, and it removes strategic hand management."

**On-Screen:** Red X over "Show All 8 Cards"

---

## Scene 3: The New System (0:25–0:50)
**Visual:** New hand with 4 cards, showing "Deck Pool" indicator

**Voiceover:**
"Now, you see only 4 cards in your hand. When you play a card, a new one is drawn from your shuffled deck. This creates strategic depth."

**Animation Sequence:**
1. Hand shows: [Knight, Archer, Baby Dragon, Giant]
2. Player plays Archer (drag animation)
3. New card draws: Skeleton Army
4. Hand updates: [Knight, Baby Dragon, Giant, Skeleton Army]

**On-Screen:** "Deck Pool: 3 cards left" + "Next: 💀"

---

## Scene 4: Deck Cycling (0:50–1:15)
**Visual:** Playing 8 cards in sequence, showing cycle count

**Voiceover:**
"Once you've played all 8 cards, the deck reshuffles automatically. You'll see the cycle counter increment. This keeps the game flowing without weird pauses."

**Animation Sequence:**
1. Cycle through 8 cards
2. On 8th card: "Cycled: 1x" appears
3. New shuffled deck draws begin
4. Game continues smoothly

**On-Screen:** Numbers counting down: "8 → 7 → 6 → 5 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 1 → RESHUFFLE → 8"

---

## Scene 5: Strategic Implications (1:15–1:45)
**Visual:** Split screen comparing deck types

**Voiceover:**
"This changes how you build decks. Fast-cycling decks (3.0–3.5 average elixir) now feel incredible. You can play 3 cards in the opening."

**Left Side:**
- Balanced Deck (3.2 avg): Green ✅
- Cards: Knight, Archer, Skeleton Army, Minions, Arrows, Cannon, Baby Dragon, Fireball
- "Feel: Fast and fun"

**Right Side:**
- Greedy Deck (4.0 avg): Yellow ⚠️
- Cards: Giant, Witch, Bomb Tower, P.E.K.K.A, 4 mid-cost
- "Feel: Slow and risky"

**On-Screen:** Deck Builder showing "✅ Elixir balanced - nice cycling deck!" vs "⚠️ High average elixir - deck may feel slow"

---

## Scene 6: Next Card Preview (1:45–2:00)
**Visual:** Showing "Next: 💀 Skeleton Army" indicator

**Voiceover:**
"You can see the next card coming. This gives you one card lookahead, so you can plan your strategy without removing all surprise."

**Animation:** Hover over "Next" indicator, show tooltip with card name + cost
