# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - Clash Royale Browser Game

## 30-Second Setup

1. **Open the game:**
   ```
   Double-click: index.html
   ```
   OR open it in your browser directly.

2. **Play immediately** - no installation, no build process needed!

## How to Play (2 Minutes)

### Main Menu
- Select difficulty: **Easy** (casual), **Medium** (balanced), or **Hard** (challenging)
- Click **"Build Deck"**

### Build Your Deck
- Choose exactly **8 cards** from the 16 available
- Each card costs 2-7 elixir
- Click cards to select (click again to deselect)
- See "Average Elixir Cost" to balance your deck
- Click **"Start Battle"** when ready

### During Battle (3 minutes)
- **Play cards** by clicking them in your hand
- Need enough **elixir** (purple bar, bottom)
- **Units move** automatically toward enemy towers
- **Towers**:
  - Destroy both Princess Towers → King Tower unlocks
  - Destroy King Tower → **WIN**
- **Spells** (Fireball, Arrows, Freeze) cast instantly
- **Double Elixir** kicks in at 60 seconds (faster regeneration)

### Win Conditions
- 🎯 Destroy enemy **King Tower** = instant victory
- 💀 Enemy **King Tower HP** ≤ 0 = win
- ⏰ Most tower HP when timer expires = win
- 🏰 If you're tied on towers at timer = tie

### After Battle
- See results: Victory, Defeat, or Tie
- Click **"Play Again"** to return to menu

| Cost | Cards |
|------|-------|

**Tip:** Mix high-cost (strong) and low-cost (quick) cards for flexibility.

## AI Difficulty Levels

### 🟢 Easy
- Bot plays every 5-8 seconds
- Random card selection
- No strategy
- **Best for:** Learning the game

### 🟡 Medium
- Bot plays every 3-5 seconds
- Some strategic plays
- Waits for elixir buildup
- **Best for:** Casual play

### 🔴 Hard
- Bot plays every 2-3 seconds
- Advanced strategy
- Active counter-plays
- **Best for:** Challenging experience

## Quick Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Balance troops and spells
- Save elixir for important moments
- Focus fire on one tower
- Use spells to clear swarms
- Play Freeze Spell when overwhelmed

❌ **DON'T:**
- Spend all elixir at once
- Play expensive cards early
- Ignore incoming attacks
- Build up units without reason
- Waste spells randomly

## Controls

- **Select Card:** Click on card in hand
- **Difficulty:** Click button on menu
- **Select Deck Cards:** Click to select, click again to deselect
- **Navigation:** Use on-screen buttons

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Game won't load | Refresh page, check internet (CDN resources) |
| Can't play card | Not enough elixir - wait for bar to fill |
| Units aren't moving | Give them time to reach targets |
| Game is laggy | Close other browser tabs |
| On mobile? | Use your browser's mobile view or open on phone |
