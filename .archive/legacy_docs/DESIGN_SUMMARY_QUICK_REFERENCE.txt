THE 5 CORE PROBLEMS & FIXES AT A GLANCE
================================================================================

❌ PROBLEM 1: Skeleton Army costs 3 elixir (too expensive for cycle card)
✅ SOLUTION: Change to 2 elixir
💥 IMPACT: Enables fast-paced cycle decks, more varied early-game strategies

❌ PROBLEM 2: Tower targeting is random (Hog Rider can ignore towers)
✅ SOLUTION: Priority system (Flying > Building-Targeters > Ground)
💥 IMPACT: Hog Rider becomes fair 4-elixir threat; towers are intelligent

❌ PROBLEM 3: Destroyed towers disappear (no visual feedback)
✅ SOLUTION: Show floating crown (👑) when tower destroyed
💥 IMPACT: Clear visual destruction feedback; satisfying moment

❌ PROBLEM 4: Elixir regen is flat (no phase pacing)
✅ SOLUTION: 3-phase regen (Early 0.015 → Mid 0.03 → Late 0.045)
💥 IMPACT: Match has distinct phases; double/triple elixir feels exciting

❌ PROBLEM 5: AI plays randomly (no strategy or counter-play)
✅ SOLUTION: 3-tier AI with personalities (Easy react, Medium counter, Hard synergize)
💥 IMPACT: Every difficulty feels intentional; meaningful progression

THE THREE-PHASE MATCH SYSTEM
================================================================================

EARLY GAME (0–60 seconds)
├─ Elixir Rate: 0.50/sec (tight, careful)
├─ Feeling: Testing phase, high stakes
├─ Strategy: Cycle rotation, establish win-con
├─ Typical Play: Skeleton Army → Arrows trades
└─ Psychological Hook: "Don't waste elixir"

MID GAME (60–120 seconds) [⚡ DOUBLE ELIXIR ⚡]
├─ Elixir Rate: 1.00/sec (tempo accelerates!)
├─ Feeling: Excitement! Opportunity window!
├─ Strategy: Build pushes, use spells, defend/counter
├─ Typical Play: Giant + Musketeer pushes
└─ Psychological Hook: "Capitalize NOW!"

LATE GAME (120–180 seconds) [🚀 TRIPLE ELIXIR 🚀]
├─ Elixir Rate: 1.50/sec (frantic, all-in)
├─ Feeling: Maximum intensity, massive plays
├─ Strategy: Multiple threats simultaneously
├─ Typical Play: Hog + Freeze + Skeleton Army spam
└─ Psychological Hook: "All cards on table!"

OVERTIME (180s+ if tied) [⚡ SUDDEN DEATH ⚡]
├─ Elixir Rate: 1.50/sec (maintains)
├─ Win Condition: First tower to take damage loses
└─ Psychological Hook: "One mistake = game over"

AI DIFFICULTY TIERS
================================================================================

EASY (Beginner-Friendly)
├─ Play Interval: 5 seconds
├─ Strategy: Reactive defense, no planning
├─ Counter-Logic: Plays defensive if you have 3+ troops
├─ Deck: Mixed (no synergy)
├─ Expected: Beginner wins 80%+
└─ Feel: Telegraphed, predictable, learning-friendly

MEDIUM (Competitive)
├─ Play Interval: 3 seconds
├─ Strategy: Counter-play, push building, smart defense
├─ Counter-Logic: Swarm → Arrows, Threats → Building defense
├─ Deck: Balanced synergy
├─ Expected: Intermediate wins 50%+
└─ Feel: Competent, strategic, fair challenge

HARD (Competitive+)
├─ Play Interval: 1.5 seconds
├─ Strategy: Phase-aware, synergy combos, aggressive
├─ Counter-Logic: Hog + Freeze combos, cycle pressure, all-in late
├─ Deck: Aggressive synergy
├─ Expected: Advanced wins 30%+
└─ Feel: Aggressive, synergistic, punishing play

================================================================================
THE META: 4 ARCHETYPES (ROCK-PAPER-SCISSORS BALANCE)
================================================================================

BEATDOWN (ROCK) - Slow, Overwhelming Push
├─ Core: Giant + Witch + ranged support
├─ Win Condition: Grind down towers
├─ Beats: Swarms (splash clears them)
└─ Loses to: Cycle (out-rotates defenses)

CYCLE (SCISSORS) - Fast, Cheap Rotation
├─ Core: Hog Rider + cheap cycle cards
├─ Win Condition: Play Hog 3-5 times before opponent defends
├─ Beats: Beatdown (cheaper counters, faster cycle)
└─ Loses to: Spell-Bait (baits waste counters)

SPELL-BAIT (PAPER) - Bait & Punish
├─ Core: Skeleton Army + ranged units
├─ Win Condition: Force spell waste; play Musketeer undefended
├─ Beats: Beatdown (multiple undefended threats)
└─ Loses to: Cycle (ignores baits, out-cycles)

CONTROL (BALANCE) - Defend & Chip
├─ Core: Bomb Tower + Cannon + spells
├─ Win Condition: Defend everything, win on counter-attacks
├─ Beats: Swarm rushes (splash defense)
└─ Loses to: Cycle (cheap breaks through cheap defense)

Meta Principle: All 4 archetypes have a matchup they beat
                and a matchup they lose to ✓ BALANCED

CARD ROLES & SYNERGIES
================================================================================

CYCLE CARDS (Play multiple times):
├─ Skeleton Army (2 elixir) - Bait spells
├─ Arrows (3 elixir) - Kill swarms
└─ Knight (3 elixir) - Small push

WIN CONDITIONS (Play 1-3 times to win):
├─ Hog Rider (4 elixir) - Fast building destroyer
├─ Giant (5 elixir) - Mega tank (needs support)
└─ PEKKA (7 elixir) - All-in finisher

SUPPORT CARDS (Complement pushes):
├─ Musketeer (4 elixir) - Ranged DPS
├─ Baby Dragon (4 elixir) - Flying splash
└─ Witch (5 elixir) - Swarm generator

DEFENSE CARDS (Stop opponent):
├─ Cannon (3 elixir) - Ground defense
└─ Bomb Tower (5 elixir) - Swarm defense

SPELLS (Instant effect):
├─ Arrows (3 elixir) - AOE clearer
├─ Fireball (4 elixir) - High damage
└─ Freeze (4 elixir) - Stops pushes, paralyzes tower

================================================================================
BALANCE PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

POWER BUDGET MODEL:
  2 elixir: ~500 HP equivalent (or 12 weak units)
  3 elixir: ~400-650 HP, 100+ damage
  4 elixir: ~400-950 HP, 100+ damage + special
  5 elixir: ~550-2000 HP, 150+ damage
  7 elixir: ~2400+ HP, 290+ damage (extreme)

PRINCIPLE 1: Every card has a counter
└─ Skeleton Army → Arrows, Hog → Cannon, Giant → Arrows+ranged

PRINCIPLE 2: No card counters everything
└─ PEKKA strong but loses to Freeze

PRINCIPLE 3: No unbeatable strategy
└─ Beatdown beats Swarms but loses to Cycle

PRINCIPLE 4: High cost = lower DPS/elixir (tradeoff)
└─ PEKKA: expensive but overwhelming
