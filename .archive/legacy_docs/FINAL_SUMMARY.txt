💡 KEY DESIGN INSIGHTS:
================================================================================

THE WINNING FORMULA:
  Strategy + Timing + Synergy + Fair Challenge = Replayability

THE THREE-PHASE ARC:
  Early:   Player tests strategies, establishes win-condition
  Mid:     OPPORTUNITY! Double elixir enables bigger plays
  Late:    CLIMAX! Triple elixir creates frantic final battles

CARD ROLES CREATE SYNERGIES:
  Cycle Cards    → Play multiple times per match
  Win Conditions → Your main threat unit
  Support Units  → Amplify your win-con
  Defense Cards  → Stop opponent's threats
  Spells         → Instant effects, high impact

THE BALANCE PHILOSOPHY:
  - Higher cost = lower DPS/elixir (tradeoff)
  - Every card has a counter
  - No card counters everything
  - All archetypes viable
